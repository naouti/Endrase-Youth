About Us

Enderase Youth Assocation

Introduction Enderase (Amharic ‘እንደራሴ’) used to express shared experience or understanding with someone else. It suggests that the speaker can relate to the feelings, thoughts, or experiences of another person because they have gone through something similar. Enderase is a way to show solidarity and support for others.

Our Mission

As Youth led assocation we have mission statement of enlightening,empowering and exposing the untapped potential of the youth to the next level in value of love,diligence and excellence.

Creating an enviroment for the youth where the youth engagemnt is secured and advancing thier most potential to the maximum level.

Equibing the emerging youg leaders across the country from rural up to urban in love and diligence where sustanavlity and development is acheived.

Our Vision

Enderase has vision of light which we want to see a youth generation that is eye opened and be light his nation & generation.

This is Vision of Light were we open the eyes of the youths to see what they have and work on their full potential in changing thier community and genration.

Our vision is a heart born sight were we speak what we see and were we do what we speak.Enderase youth assocation has been dedicated since the offical opening time up to now in making this to happen.

Programs and Projects
Personal & Community Development Service (PCDS)
Volunteerism, Youth Health, Humanitarian & Charity
Working Areas and more

Personal and community development services refer to a range of activities, programs, and initiatives that aim to enhance the well-being, skills, and capacities of individuals and communities. These services focus on empowering individuals to improve their personal lives and supporting communities to thrive collectively.

Leadership Diplomacy Program (LEAD)
SDG Advocacy, Youth Leadership, Emerging Diplomats, Culture Exchange
Working Areas and more

Leadership and diplomacy are two distinct but interconnected concepts that relate to the realm of governance, influence, and international relations. Leadership pertains to guiding and influencing others towards a common goal, while diplomacy involves managing international relations and resolving conflicts through peaceful means. Both concepts require strong communication skills, strategic thinking, and the ability to navigate complex relationships to achieve positive outcomes.

Employment Entrepreneurship Path (EEP)
PreCareer Workshop, Job Readiness, Entrepreneurship Skills
Working Areas and more

Entrepreneurship and employment paths represent two different approaches to work and career. Entrepreneurship offers individuals the opportunity to pursue their own visions, be their own boss, and potentially achieve financial success. It requires a high degree of self-motivation, risk-taking, and the ability to handle uncertainty. Employment, on the other hand, provides stability, a predictable income, and the opportunity to specialize in a particular field. It offers a structured career path, benefits, and the potential for advancement within an organization.
