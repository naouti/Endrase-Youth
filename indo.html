Enderase Youth Association

Enderase Youth Assocation is a youth-led group that works on projects to empower young people in Ethiopia. It has over 600 volunteers across the country.

Our Impactful Events

Dinq wege conference

The last Dinq Wege program featured Abel Sahlu, founder of New Hub Art School, and Biruk, founder of Twinkles and professional photographer. They shared valuable experiences and insights.

Successful Blood Donation Drive

Executives, teams, and volunteers joined a Jan 4, 2024 blood drive at the Ethiopian Blood Bank during Giving for Living Week, drawing a sizable turnout as people eagerly supported this noble cause.

Introduction Enderase (Amharic ‘እንደራሴ’) used to express shared experience or understanding with someone else. It suggests that the speaker can relate to the feelings, thoughts, or experiences of another person because they have gone through something similar. Enderase is a way to show solidarity and support for others.
