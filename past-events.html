CCRDA training

Date:December 28-30,2023

Veneue:Addis Ababa

Enderase Youth Association President, Biruk, has emerged successful in a three-day training program focused on "Resource Mobilization & Capacity Building". The training, held in the vibrant city of Adama, was organized by the Christian Conestrium Relief Development Association (CCRDA). The program specifically focused on empowering participants to pursue their organizational vision irrespective of donor dependencies or limited resources. During the training, Biruk effectively shared his knowledge and experiences in harnessing local resources for various projects. He emphasized the significant impact of the Enderase volunteers network in achieving successful resource mobilization outcomes. Expressing his gratitude,The President extended sincere thanks to the Christian Conestrium Relief Development Association and Redeem the Generation.
